🌟 What is Budget Buddy?

Welcome to Budget Buddy! 🎉 It's a super fun web app designed! Learn how to manage your allowance, save for awesome toys, and keep track of your spending. Think of it as your very own piggy bank coach! 💰

📚 How to Use Budget Buddy

  1. Click the link or scan the QR code to open Budget Buddy.
  2. Enter how much money you get each week. 💵
  3. Write down what you buy — like snacks, toys, or games! 🍭
  4. Set fun goals for saving, like a new puzzle or bike! 🚲
  5. Check how much you're saving and feel proud! 🎊
