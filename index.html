🌟 What is Budget Buddy?

🎉 It's a super fun web app designed! Learn how to manage your allowance, save for awesome toys, and keep track of your spending. Think of it as your very own piggy bank coach! 💰

📚 How to Use Budget Buddy

  1. ✅ Step 1: Go to the App
    • 🌐 Open your web browser
    • 👉 Type in: http://budget-buddy-ui.github.io/bb/index.html or scan the QR code below.
  2. ✅ Step 2: Register (If You’re New)
    • 🆕 Click ✨ New Buddy? Sign Up!
    • ✍️ Fill in your nickname, email, and password
    • ✅ Click 🌟 Let's Go!
  3. Step 3: Log In
    • 📧 Enter your email and password
    • 🚪 Click 🚀 Come On In! to go to your dashboard.
  4. ✅ Step 4: Add Your Allowance
    • 💵 On your dashboard, find the Today's Money Mission! section
    • 📝 Type on how much allowance you received.
  5. ✅ Step 5: Track Your Spending
    • 🛒 Go to the Expenses section
    • 📝 Input all of your expenses
    • ✅ Click Save and let the app do the math!
  6. ✅ Step 6: Check Today's Money Story!
    • 📊 Go to the Dashboard
    • 📅 See how much you earned, spent, and saved today
    • 🏁 Use it to reach your savings goals!
  7. ✅ Step 7: See Your 📜 My Money Journey History!
    • 📚 Click on History or My Journey section
    • 👀 Look at all the things you've earned and spent
    • 🎉 See how much progress you've made over time!
